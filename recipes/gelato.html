Gelato

Authentic Gelato

Italian Gelato is a frozen creamy dessert that is made with heavy cream, milk, and sugar. Gelato is very popular in Italy and can be found is almost every corner.

Directions

  1. Mix milk and cream in a medium saucepan and warm until foam forms the edges. Then remove from heat.
  2. Beat egg yolks and sugar in a large bowl until frothy. Gradually add the warm milk into the bowl, making sure to whisk constantly. Return the mixture to the saucepan; cook over medium heat, stirring until the mixture coats the back of the spoon
  3. Add the optional vanilla extract or lemon zest
  4. Strain the mixture and let it chill overnight
  5. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions. Transfer to a seal container and freeze until it reaches destired consistency.
